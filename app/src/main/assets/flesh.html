Eggs

Most people know that eggs are very healthy. Not only that — they’re also a great source of high-quality protein. Getting enough protein is very important for building bones and muscle, as well as maintaining good overall health.A medium-sized egg typically contains 5.53 g of protein and only 63 Calories.
